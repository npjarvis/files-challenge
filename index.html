Donnie Darko

Donnie Darko is an erratic teenager from Middle Sex, Virginia, growing up in the 1980s. One night Donnie is visited by a giant bunny rabbit, named Frank, that only he can see. That same night a jet engine falls on Donnie's room, destroying everything he owns. Donnie, under Frank's influential spell, gains an interest in time travel and begins studying the Philosphy of Time Travel. Donnie commits crimes, starts exhibiting anti-social behavior, and subsequently, is diagnosed with a serious case of paranoid schizophrenia.

Donnie is the main character of the 2001 film Donnie Darko.
